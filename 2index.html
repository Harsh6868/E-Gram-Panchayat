A "Gram Panchayat" is a basic governing body at the village level in India,
essentially acting as a village council,where elected members represent the community and are
responsible for managing local development issues likesanitation,water supply, and other essential
services within the village;the head of a Gram Panchayat is called a "Sarpanch" or "Pradhan"
